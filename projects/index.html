Pure JavaScript Brick Breaker

The entire game is in 2 files, one HTML and one JS. You can visit the GitHub repository to look at the code.

HistTsunami

HistTsunami was a simple website designed to help you flood your history, even with bookmarklets blocked. HistTsunami worked by spam-opening tabs of the chosen URL with different hashes. If you chose to flood your history with "https://example.com" 5 times, it'd open "https://example.com/#1" through "https://example.com/#5".
